IMAS Mockup

IMAS (Intelligent Mobility Assistant System) is a state-of-the-art solution designed to enhance driver safety and vehicle management. Through an intuitive dashboard and dedicated device interface, IMAS provides real-time alerts and critical data. Key features include advanced Drowsiness Detection, Lane Departure Warning to prevent unintended lane changes, and Blind Spot Detection for safer maneuvering. It also offers proactive Collision Warning to mitigate accidents, and Traffic Sign Recognition for improved adherence to road rules. Drivers benefit from integrated Speedometer and precise GPS Location tracking, along with immediate Emergency features for critical situations, making every journey safer and more informed.

Advanced Sensor Fusion
