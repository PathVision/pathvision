Drowsiness Detection

Detects signs of driver fatigue by monitoring eye and head movements. This helps prevent accidents caused by drowsiness during long or late-night drives.

Lane Departure Warning

Warns the driver when the vehicle begins to drift out of its lane unintentionally. This system helps maintain proper lane discipline and avoids side collisions

Collision Warning

Monitors the road ahead and warns the driver of potential collisions. It ensures timely braking and reduces the chances of front-end accidents.

GPS Location

Provides accurate navigation and real-time tracking of the vehicle. It supports route planning, location sharing, and emergency assistance.

Blind Spot Detection

Detects vehicles in the driver’s blind spots and provides timely warnings. This reduces the risk of collisions during lane changes or turns.

Traffic Sign Recognition

Identifies and displays road signs to keep drivers informed and compliant. It helps avoid traffic violations and improves road awareness.

Speedometer

Displays the vehicle’s real-time speed to promote safe and legal driving. It helps drivers maintain control and avoid over-speeding.

Emergency Response

Automatically contacts emergency services in the event of an accident. It ensures quick response, improving safety and saving lives.
