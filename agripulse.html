AgriPulse-360 Mockup

AgriPulse-360 is a comprehensive agricultural platform designed to empower farmers with data-driven insights and smart tools. From real-time crop health monitoring and weather forecasting to efficient resource management and market access, AgriPulse-360 consolidates essential functionalities into one intuitive interface. It offers features like intelligent crop recommendations, detailed soil health analysis (both manual and sensor-based), smart water management, and access to government schemes and subsidies. Additionally, farmers can benefit from a dedicated market space, financial tracking, smart pest detection, and an AI-powered Agri Assistant, along with a rich library of educational resources to foster continuous learning and improve productivity.
