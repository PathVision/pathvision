Dashboard Overview

A comprehensive dashboard providing a quick glance at essential agricultural metrics. Users can monitor crop health, weather forecasts, and market trends, all from a centralized, easy-to-navigate interface. This allows for informed decision-making at a glance.

Crop Management

Efficiently manage your crops with this dedicated section. Track growth stages, apply treatments, and schedule tasks to optimize yield and health. The interface provides detailed insights and tools for effective cultivation practices.

Crop Recommendation

Receive intelligent crop recommendations tailored to your specific soil conditions and local climate. This feature utilizes data analytics to suggest the most suitable crops for maximizing productivity and sustainability, ensuring optimal returns on your efforts.

Manual Soil Health Analysis

Conduct detailed manual soil health analysis to understand your farm's foundation. Input various parameters to get insights into nutrient levels, pH, and composition, empowering you to make precise fertilization and amendment decisions for healthier crops.

Sensor-Based Soil Health Analysis

Leverage cutting-edge sensor technology for real-time soil health monitoring. Sensors provide continuous data on moisture, temperature, and nutrient availability, allowing for dynamic adjustments and optimized resource management directly impacting crop vitality.

Smart Water Management

Optimize water usage with smart management features. This system helps schedule irrigation based on weather forecasts, soil moisture levels, and crop needs, minimizing waste and ensuring your plants receive the right amount of water at the right time.

Schemes and Subsidies

Stay informed about relevant government schemes and agricultural subsidies. This section provides up-to-date information on financial aid, grants, and support programs available to farmers, helping you access beneficial resources and enhance your farming operations.

Weather Forecasting

Accurate and localized weather forecasts to help you plan your agricultural activities. Get timely updates on temperature, rainfall, and other crucial weather patterns, enabling you to protect your crops and schedule tasks effectively, minimizing risks.

Market Space

Connect with buyers and sellers in the dedicated market space. This platform facilitates direct trade of agricultural produce, fostering fair prices and expanding market reach for farmers, helping them sell their products efficiently and profitably.

Crop Financial Tracking

Track your crop-related finances with ease. Monitor expenses, revenues, and profitability for each crop cycle, gaining clear insights into your agricultural business's financial health. This tool helps in better budgeting and financial planning.

Smart Pest Detection

Utilize smart pest detection to identify and manage pests early. This feature employs advanced techniques to recognize pest infestations, providing recommendations for effective and environmentally friendly control methods, safeguarding your crops from damage.

Agri Assistant

Your personalized agricultural assistant providing tailored advice and support. From answering queries about specific crops to troubleshooting farming challenges, this AI-powered assistant is designed to be a reliable knowledge base and problem solver for farmers.

Educational Resources

Access a rich library of educational content on various farming practices, new technologies, and agricultural trends. This section is dedicated to empowering farmers with knowledge, helping them to continuously improve their skills and adopt innovative solutions.
